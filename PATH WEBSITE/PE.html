Silent Witnesses

In remembrance of those who have lost their lives to domestic violence IC Prevention and Peers Advocating Towards Health (P.A.T.H.) have placed red life size silhouettes across campus. These silhouettes are called "Silent Witnesses' ", each one represents an Illinois resident who has lost their life to domestic violence. The Silent Witness' are located in Lincoln Hall, Caine Student Center, Kirby Hall, Baxter Hall, and Brunner Athletic Center. Each one has a purple story shield on their chest with information about their lives, their abuse, and what, if any, criminal justice remedy their death has received. Nationwide, an average of 3 women are killed by a current or former intimate partner every day.
