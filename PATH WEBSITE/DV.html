What is Domestic Violence?

A pattern of abusive behavior in any relationship that is used by one person to gain or maintain power and control over another.


Domestic Violence can be shown by:

  1. Physical Abuse
  2. Sexual Abuse
  3. Emotional Abuse
  4. Economic Abuse
  5. Psychological Abuse
  6. Technological Abuse
