What is Sexual Assault?

Sexual contact or behavior without explicit consent of the victim.


Sexual Assault can be shown by:

  1. Attempted rape
  2. Fondling or unwanted sexual touching
  3. Forcing a victim to perform sexual acts, such as oral sex or penetrating the perpetrator's body
  4. Penetration of the victim's body (rape)
